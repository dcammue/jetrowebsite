About Us

Our Values

Our Principles

  1. 1. To provide young Liberians with life-changing technological skills.

  2. 2. To help build the world we live in.

  3. 3. We want to use the people's software to support the people's movement in our world.

  4. 4. we want to breed the new generation of software Developers in Liberia, and Africa by an extension.

Worker Co-Op

As a young coop we want to grow by establishing long lasting relationships with our partners. Our team is composed of young, skilled and very creative Front-End and Back-End Developers and graphic designers, with a few old timers sprinkled in to flavor the mix. We know that together we can do so much than doing it alone, and we're really willing and happy to collaborate with other organizations, progressive groups and partners.

Free and Open Source

JETRO Web Development is committed to using open source software throughout our project. This both aligns with our values, and protects our clients from being forced into a monopoly relationship with the owners of the tools we deliver. Development and maintenance of the entire set of tools delivered by us can be performed by anyone with the skills to do so, without any legal restriction.
